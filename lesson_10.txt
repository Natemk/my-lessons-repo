Why Modules?

Professional software is not written in one script.

Its written across multiple differenmt files. 

This helps us 
1. mantain our code,
2. debug our code,
3. allows us to collaborate.

SO its simply a Python file.

Aliases:

Module names can be too long:

Use Aliases

e.g

import calculator as calc

print(calc.add(4, 5))

e.g 2:

import math as m

print(m.sqrt(25))
